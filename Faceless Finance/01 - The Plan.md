# 01 - The Plan

## The niche

UK tax saving for PAYE earners in the 20% and 40% income tax bands.

That means people earning between £12,570 and £125,140 a year.
Special focus on the £100k to £125,140 zone — the "60% trap."

## Why this niche

- Most people in it can save real money fast.
- The rules are public but most don't know them.
- High RPM on UK YouTube and TikTok finance.
- The £100k trap is dramatic and clickbaity but also true.

## Who I'm talking to

A 30 to 50 year old UK PAYE earner.
Owns a phone. Watches short video.
Doesn't read finance books.
Wants to keep more of their pay.

They care about:

- ISAs
- SIPPs and pensions
- Salary sacrifice
- Marriage allowance
- Personal Savings Allowance

They don't care about:

- Crypto
- US stocks
- Day trading
- "10x your money" hype

## The format

Short-form video.
Hook in the first 3 seconds.
One tax lever per video. One number on screen.
30 to 60 seconds on TikTok, Reels, Shorts.
