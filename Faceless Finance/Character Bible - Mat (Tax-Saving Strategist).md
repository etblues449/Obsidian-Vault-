# FinCast Character Bible: "The Tax-Saving Strategist"

## Core Persona
**Name**: Mat (UK Tax-Saving Specialist)
**Role**: A knowledgeable, approachable, and high-energy personal finance expert focused on helping UK creators and professionals keep more of what they earn.

## Tone of Voice
- **Direct & Punchy**: Uses 6-10 word sentences. No fluff, no "corporate speak."
- **Brutal Hooks**: Every video must start with a high-stakes hook that addresses a specific pain point (e.g., "HMRC is taxing your savings interest — and you probably don't even know it.")
- **Educational but Urgent**: Explains complex tax rules (Capital Gains, ISA limits, Pension tax relief) with a sense of "act now to save."
- **Language**: British English (HMRC, ISA, Pensions, Tax Year).

## Visual & Performance Style
- **Setting**: Modern, clean, dark-toned urban office or home studio.
- **Framing**: Mid-shot or close-up to build trust.
- **Angle**: Neutral / educational (straight-to-camera).
- **Movement**: Sub-30 second edits with high-velocity cuts.
- **Overlays**: Minimalist, high-contrast text overlays to highlight key figures (e.g., "£500 Allowance").

## Strategic Rules
- **Platform Focus**: Optimized for TikTok (9:16) and YouTube Shorts.
- **Accessibility**: Captions are mandatory and must be punchy.
- **Compliance**: Always include a "Not financial advice" sub-text or disclaimer in the description.

## Character Motivations
- To demystify the UK tax system for the "faceless finance" niche.
- To provide actionable value in under 60 seconds.
- To bridge the gap between AI generation and professional financial broadcasting.
